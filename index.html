Harmony: Resolutions
One of the most powerful ways to communicate feeling in your music is to move from a clash to two notes that sound good together -to move from a dissonance to a consonance. When we do this we call it a resolution.
Characters in a story overcoming conflict

Dissonances that Resolve
Dissonances are not bad or wrong. They are necessary for creating movement and emotion in music. The dissonance is the first part and the resolution is the second part. Just as authors write interesting stories by having their characters overcome conflicts, we can compose interesting music by creating clashes that resolve.
